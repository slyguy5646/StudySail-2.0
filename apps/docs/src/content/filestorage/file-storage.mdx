### What file types are supported?

For now, PDF files are the only format supported. (Docx, RTF, Markdown, and more coming soon)

<br />
<br />

### Uploading Files

(Before following these steps, [log in](https://studysail.com/sign-in) or [create a new account](https://studysail.com/sign-up))

{/* step 1 */}
<br />

<Step number="1" title="Navigate to the uploader" />
<br />

First, [visit the "Add New" section of your dashboard](https://studysail.com/dashboard/add-new).

<ImageWithCaption
  src="https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/blog-files-d9a40.appspot.com/o/filedropzone.png?alt=media&token=5ae10231-1c21-4f8d-9553-38af100001f7"
  alt="Image of StudySail file dropzone"
/>

{/* step 2 */}
<br />

<Step number="2" title="Select a file" />
<br />

To queue a file uploading to StudySail, drag and drop a file into the uploader box or click on the box to open your computer's file explorer. As mentioned above, PDF is the only file format currently supported (more coming soon).

{/* step 3 */}
<br />

<Step number="3" title="Upload" />
<br />

Finally, click the upload button and wait for the upload to complete. (Leaving the page before the upload is complete will cause the upload to fail) After the upload is complete you will be automatically redirected to your new StudySail document.

<ImageWithCaption
  src="https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/blog-files-d9a40.appspot.com/o/fileinuploader.png?alt=media&token=0650cbba-555f-46cd-a7fe-18e029bb6e59"
  alt="Image of StudySail file dropzone"
/>

{/* step 4 */}
<br />

<Step number="4" title="Done!" />
<br />

After uploading, your file is safely stored in StudySail allowing your to use it to its full potential. If you have any questions, feel free to [send us a message](https://twitter.com/messages/compose?recipient_id=1679959524310958081).
